About Us

About us We are aimed at providing free platform to all to exchange their ideas in terms of books .
Books are our buddy and also our teacher , teaching us to think .
And main is to help us Generate Ideas .
This platform is similar to any offline second hand book store except the fact that it earns ZERO . Yes , literally 0 .
Since this website is Charity based that is it is based on concept of No Profit and No Loss " .
Our services are based on Donation and Request .
If we are having more donation then it would help us to fulfil more requests .
By this platform we aimed at
1. Giving books to needed person .
2. Saving Trees by reusing books and maintaining the book flow.
3. Saving environment by decreasing demand for new books.
4. Above all of this we are giving smile to that one face that requires book and giving satisfaction to that one who donated the book.
